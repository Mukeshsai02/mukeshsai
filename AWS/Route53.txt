DNS:-
It is the backbone of the internet,which translates urls,hostnames into ipaddress.

Route 53:-
It is a domain registrar,where u can register ur own domains like 'example.com'

Route 53 - Hosted Zones:-
It is used to define how to route traffic to a domain & sub-domain.

public hosted zone:- It contains records that specify how to route traffic on the internet.
private hosted zone:- It contains records that specify how to route traffic within one or more vpcs.
